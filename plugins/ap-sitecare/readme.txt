=== AP SiteCare Reporter ===
Contributors: adrielpartners
Requires at least: 6.0
Requires PHP: 8.0
Stable tag: 0.1.0

Securely reports WordPress operational health to the AP SiteCare dashboard.

== Installation ==

1. Upload and activate the plugin.
2. Open Settings > AP SiteCare.
3. Enter the dashboard URL, Site ID, and Site Secret issued by AP SiteCare.
4. Save settings and test the connection.
